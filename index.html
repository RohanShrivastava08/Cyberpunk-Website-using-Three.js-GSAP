About Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world, action-adventure RPG set in Night City, a megalopolis obsessed with power, glamour and body modification.

You play as V, a mercenary outlaw going after a one-of-a-kind implant that is the key to immortality. You can customize your character's cyberware, skillset and playstyle, and explore a vast city where the choices you make shape the story and the world around you.

Official

CD PROJEKT RED

Established in 2002, located in Warsaw (HQ), Kraków and Wrocław, Poland, CD PROJEKT RED was born out of raw enthusiasm for video games.

The studio's founders: Michał Kiciński and Marcin Iwiński, both pioneers in video game distribution in Poland in the 90s, were ready to take on the challenge of developing their own games.

Welcome to the Dark Future

In 2077, America is in pieces. Megacorporations control life in all its aspects from the top floors of their fortresses. Down below, drug-pushing gangs, tech hustlers, and illegal braindance slingers run the streets.

Night City

A megalopolis that offers violence and oppression, as well as opportunity and glamour.

Corporations
